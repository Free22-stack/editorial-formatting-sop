## 1.0 Scope

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) applies to the formatting and structural
elements of both educational and marketing/sales articles. It provides clear
guidelines for fonts, titles, images, and other formatting considerations to
ensure readability, engagement, and consistency across published content.

---

## 2.0 Purpose

The purpose of this SOP is to establish a standardized set of formatting
guidelines for creating educational and marketing/sales articles. These
guidelines help maintain a professional and visually appealing format while
optimizing readability and capturing the attention of the intended audience.

## 4.0 Policy Guidelines

### 4.1 Fonts

- Use sans-serif fonts for educational and marketing/sales articles  
- Recommended fonts include **Arial** and **Montserrat**  
- Adjust font weight or line thickness as needed for readability  
- Font size should be **11-point or 12-point**, depending on the font style  

---

### 4.2 Citation Style

- The **Chicago citation style** must be used  

**Example citation format:**  
Author Full Name, *Book Title: Subtitle*, edition.  
(Place of publication: Publisher, Year), page numbers, URL.

---

### 4.3 Margins

- Maintain a standard **1-inch margin** on all sides for consistent formatting  

---

### 4.4 Length

- There is no strict word or page limit  
- Articles should remain **concise, informative, and focused**  

---

### 4.5 Titles

- Titles should be **center-justified** for visual consistency  
- Title font size should be **14-point or 16-point**, depending on the font style  
- Use the same font as the main body text unless differentiation is required  

---

### 4.6 Images

- Images should be appropriately sized for the content and platform  
- Ensure clarity and visual appeal  
- Use text wrapping to position images effectively within the article  
- Avoid excessive image use; include images only when they add value  
- Image borders may be used for visual distinction when appropriate  
- If borders are used, select colors that complement the overall design  
- Copy and paste the **source link for all images** at the bottom of the article  

---
